This Refund Policy applies to MacWrap Premium subscriptions. Payments and refunds are processed by our Merchant of Record, Lemon Squeezy.

Free tier

The core of MacWrap — wrapping your own .exe files into Mac apps — is free. There is nothing to refund on the free tier.

14-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with MacWrap Premium, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your first subscription payment, for any reason. This also reflects the EU consumer right of withdrawal.

Renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically. You can cancel anytime from the app (⚙️ → Subscription) or via the customer portal in your purchase email; you keep access until the end of the paid period. Renewal payments are generally non-refundable once the new period has started, except where required by law — but if something went wrong (e.g. you were charged after cancelling, or the service was unavailable), contact us and we will make it right.

Honesty about compatibility

MacWrap tells you the expected compatibility tier before you commit, and Premium is a subscription to the whole curated catalog and its updates — not a single app. If a specific app cannot be made to work, you still receive every other recipe. If you feel the service did not deliver what was promised, the 14-day guarantee has you covered.

How to request a refund

Email support@macwrap.app from the address used to subscribe, or use the refund option in your Lemon Squeezy receipt. Refunds are issued to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days.
